all about tokki

tokki is a maltipoo, with beige brown hair. maltipoos are different than other dogs because they are hypoallergenic, so they dont shed!
That is why we say that maltipoos grow hair instead of fur, which sheds.
tokki is a very special dog for a myriad of reasons:

  1. she's small, her appetite is bigger than her body
  2. she loves to use her voice and is not afraid to shout...
  3. she makes a sort of nasally gremlin growl sometimes and it's not determined if it's out of anger or fun..
  4. she can transform, making her a master of disguise

tokki's likes and dislikes

likes:

  • celery
  • carrots
  • jumping and hopping around
  • eating hair
  • her wide variety of toys

dislikes:

  • getting bored
  • being held when she doenst want to
  • being ignored
